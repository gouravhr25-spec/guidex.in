About GuideX

A recruitment firm built around fit, not pressure.

GuideX started as a freelance recruitment effort and grew into a focused hiring company with one mission: guide India's unemployed youth toward the right career, not just any job.

Before suggesting a role, we understand skills, communication comfort, shift preference, salary expectation, city preference, and work mode. That is how guidance becomes practical.

We started with no office, no investors, and no safety net. Just a phone, discipline, and an obsession with placing the right people.
Gourav Pandey, Founder
